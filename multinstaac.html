Add and manage multiple Instagram accounts on same device

It is sometimes important to have double Instagram accounts Because

.Maybe two people share the same device

.Maybe you want one of your account not to be known by others for any of your reason

.whatever reason you wnat to have multiple instagram accounts, here is the right place

Let's go

From your instagram profile page

Tap the gear icon for iphone users

Tap the three horizontal lines at the top right of your screen Android Users

Scroll down to an option "Add Account" and tap that option

Add your other account by Username and Password and you're all done

It is so Simple

Switching Accounts

To toggle between both profiles, hold down your profile picture in the navigation bar to view all connected accounts
