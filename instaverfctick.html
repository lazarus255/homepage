How To get a Verification Tick on Instagram

What is Verified Account meaning

.It means that your account is authentic presence of a notable public figure such as celebrity or global brand

.The budge was designed to make the real accounts stand out. So as to make sure instagram users follow the right person

Let's Go

Go to Instagram, vist your profile and tap the hamburger icon in the right corner

Tap Settings

Tap on Request verification

Fill the application form by

.Your legal name

.Your known as or working name (if applicable)

.Select your category industry fore example (blogger, influencer, sports, news/media, business/brand/organization,)

You can also need to submit a photo of your official goverment ID. for individuals, that could be a drivers license or passport

For business, a utability bill, an official business document, or tax fillings will do

Tap Send

Results

After Instagram reviews your apllication, you'll recieve a response in your notification tab

Within few days or atleast a week, you will recieve a direct YES or NO

the NO message looks like this

"Your account wasn't verified because it doesn't meet the criteria for verification. You can submit another request in 30days"

While a YES message looks like this

"Your account was verified. You'll now see a blue verified badge next to your username. To keep your verified status you need to have a public account, a profile picture and atleast one post. You can add extra security in your account by turning on two factor authentication"

That's All

If you request fails for the first time don't give try again and again
